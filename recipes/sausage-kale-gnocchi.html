Sausage, kale and gnocchi one-pot

Description

Plate up this delicious one-pot of sausage, kale and gnocchi in just 20 minutes, with just five minutes prep. Midweek suppers never got so easy â€“ or tasty!

I used to make this all the time at Alexandra using real sausages, but you can easily switch the Richmond Veggie sausages to make it veggie.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 pork sausages
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds (optional)
  • 500g fresh gnocchi
  • 500ml chicken stock (fresh if you can get it)
  • 100g chopped kale
  • 40g parmesan, finely grated

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large high-sided frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Squeeze the sausages straight from their skins into the pan, then use the back of a wooden spoon to break the meat up.
  3. Sprinkle in the chilli flakes and fennel seeds, if using, then fry until the sausage meat is crisp around the edges.
  4. Remove from the pan with a slotted spoon.
  5. Tip the gnocchi into the pan, fry for a minute or so, then pour in the chicken stock.
  6. Once bubbling, cover the pan with a lid and cook for 3 mins, then stir in the kale.
  7. Cook for 2 mins more or until the gnocchi is tender and the kale has wilted.
  8. Stir in the parmesan, then season with black pepper and scatter the crisp sausage meat over the top.
